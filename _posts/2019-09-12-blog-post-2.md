As of tonight, a new version of the manuscript "On the well-posedness of Bayesian inverse problems" is
[available in the arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1902.10257).
I have added some new results concerning stability in the Wasserstein distance and considered the case of 
infinite-dimensional data spaces with additive Gaussian noise.
