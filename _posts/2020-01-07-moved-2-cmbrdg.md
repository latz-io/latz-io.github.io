After graduating from my PhD programme at Technical University of Munich last December, I recently moved to University of Cambridge for a postdoc. Here, I will be part of Carola-Bibiane Schönlieb's Cambridge Image Analysis group. I will be working in the EPSRC project PET++ on new methods for image reconstruction in positron emission tomography (PET).
